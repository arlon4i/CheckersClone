How do I contact Checkers?

Dial our toll-free number on 0800 33 33 85 and speak to one of our friendly Checkline consultants or use our feedback form on the app.

Checkline operates Mondays-Saturdays from 08:00-17:00 and on Sundays and public holidays from 09:00-14:00. Please be advised that cellular rates may apply when calling from a cell phone.
