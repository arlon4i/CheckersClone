How can I share Checkers EeziCoupons and Specials with others?

To share an individual EeziCoupon, press on the specific EeziCoupon and press "Share" below the EeziCoupon description. You will be directed to a screen where you can share EeziCoupons with others via BBM, Email, Facebook, Twitter or SMS.
