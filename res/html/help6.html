What can I do if an error occurs with my EeziCoupon WiCode?

Under "Help & Contact Us" press the option "Talk to Us". If you would like to speak directly to a Checkline Consultant, please press "Speak to a Checkline Consultant" and you will be directed to the option to call one of our friendly Checkline consultants on 0800 33 33 85.

If you would prefer to send us an email, please press "Send Us a Message" and you will be directed to a screen where you are able to submit a query. Complete the form and one of our friendly Checkline consultants will contact you. Alternatively, you can contact a store directly by pressing "Contact a Store" and you will be directed to a list where you are able to select a specific store and access its contact details.
