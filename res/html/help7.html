What is My List?

Your list is your personal shopping list which you can add your preferred items to that have EeziCoupon savings. Your list helps you keep track of the relevant items that you would like to purchase in store. Swipe the screen to the left to access "My List" or press the profile icon and press "My Lists" to access your personal list.

You have the option of redeeming the coupons on your list by pressing "Redeem" which will direct you to your EeziCoupon WiCode. You do not have to add items to "My List" to redeem them.
